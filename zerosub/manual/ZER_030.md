## Overview

zerosub is a professional subtitle generation tool designed for those who want to optimize their video presentations. Our goal is to provide an efficient solution that not only meets all your requirements but also exceeds them.

## Detailed Content

### What is zerosub?

A powerful tool for generating subtitles, zerosub takes care of the timecodes and metadata needed in order to ensure seamless synchronization between your original audio tracks and subtitle tracks. With a variety of customizable options available, we aim to cater to even the most demanding clients across various industries.

### Core Features

- **Subtitle generation**: Generates high-quality subtitles from both video and audio files.
- **Timecode conversion**: Translates timecodes accurately for seamless synchronization in different projects.
- **Metadata management**: Includes automatic extraction of project metadata like title, artist, and director information.
- **Customizable settings**: Offers a wide range of customization options to accommodate diverse requirements.

### How to Use

1. Step 1: Import your video file or audio files into the tool.
2. Step 2: Adjust settings such as subtitle language, font size, style, and more for maximum presentation consistency.
3. Step 3: Generate subtitles with a click of a button.
4. Step 4: Export your finalized subtitles in various formats like MP4, AVI, or TXT for easy distribution.

### Best Practices

- **Consistency**: Ensure consistent formatting across all generated subtitles to maintain professionalism and credibility.
- **Feedback**: Use feedback from clients to refine the tool's features and user experience further.
- **Security**: Encrypt sensitive information during subtitle generation to protect client privacy.
