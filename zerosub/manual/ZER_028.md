### What is zerosub?

zerosub is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields.

### Core Features

- **Subtitle Generation**: Ability to generate high-quality subtitles for videos, ensuring smooth playback and accurate translation.
- **Subtitle Management**: Comprehensive tools for managing video subtitles, including adding new subcriptions, deleting existing ones, and updating existing entries.
- **Advanced Settings**: Allow users to customize subtitle settings such as font size, color, language, and more.

### How to Use

1. Step 1: Click on the "Add Subtitles" button in your video editor.
2. Step 2: Choose a subtitle file or import directly from the video.
3. Step 3: Select the appropriate language for subtitles (optional).
4. Step 4: Adjust settings according to your preferences (e.g., font size, color, language).
5. Step 5: Click "Save" to finalize and apply changes.

### Best Practices

- **Always keep subtitles up-to-date**: Regularly update subtitles for videos to maintain accuracy and relevancy.
- **Customize for specific languages**: If not translated in the video's native language, ensure subtitles are accurately translated for international audiences.
- **Use sublanguage settings if available**: Enhance experience with subtitles by selecting a sublanguage if provided.
