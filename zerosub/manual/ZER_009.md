### What is zerosub?

zerosub is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields such as subtitles and video subtitling. It offers various features to ensure efficient video subtitle management. 

### Core Features

- **Subtitle Creation**: Generates accurate and relevant video subtitles based on text input.
- **Video Subtitle Management**: Stores, indexes, and manages video subtitles for ease of use during playback.
- **Feature Implementation**: Includes advanced tools like time adjustment, language translation, and more.

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Create a Subtitle
1. Enter the desired text into the subtitle creation area.
2. Adjust the time stamp if necessary.
3. Choose your preferred language for translation or localization.

#### Step 2: Manage Subtitles
- View and edit existing subtitles directly from the video player.
- Batch manage subtitles across multiple videos.
- Delete unwanted subtitles with ease using the search function.

#### Step 3: Enhance Your Subtitle Experience
- Customize subtitle appearance, including font selection, size adjustments, and more.
- Share your subtitles for others to enjoy or download them easily.
