### What is zerosub?

zerosub is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields.

#### Core Features

- Simplify and organize your subtitles to make them easy to read
- Auto-generate subtitles for any video format and language automatically
- Manage subtitles with ease, allowing you to add, delete, update or reorder them as needed
- Keep your video content consistent in terms of timing, volume, tone, and style

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Select a subtitle template from the available templates

1. Go to the "Subtitle Templates" section on the main page.
2. Browse through the templates provided and select one that meets your requirements.

#### Step 2: Add or edit subtitles for each video in your project

3. Once you have selected a template, click on "Add Video" or "Edit Video" to open the editing interface.
4. Drag and drop each subtitle onto its respective clip within the video timeline.
5. Adjust the start time and end time of each subtitle as necessary using the video's timeline.
6. Preview your subtitles by clicking "Preview" at the top of the screen.

#### Step 3: Save and preview your project

7. After completing all edits, click "Save" to finalize changes.
8. Once saved, you can preview your project in full-screen mode or on a mobile device using the embedded player.

### Best Practices

- **Consistency:** Ensure that subtitles are consistent with the timing, volume, tone, and style of your video content
- **User Experience:** Aim for an intuitive user interface that makes it easy to manage and edit subtitles
- **Feedback:** Monitor user feedback to identify areas for improvement in the tool's functionality and design
