### Technology Innovation 2: Load Balancing for High Availability

To achieve high availability, we implemented a load balancing mechanism using HAProxy. This allows us to distribute incoming traffic across multiple instances of our application, ensuring that no single point of failure can bring down the entire system. By configuring HAProxy with a least-connections algorithm and a maximum number of requests per instance, we can maintain an average response time of less than 500ms even under heavy load.

### Technology Innovation 3: Hystrix Circuit Breaker for Fault Tolerance

To ensure that our application remains fault-tolerant in the presence of errors or timeouts, we implemented a Hystrix circuit breaker. This allows us to detect when a dependency (such as a database query) is taking too long to complete and automatically short-circuit the request before it times out. By configuring Hystrix with a timeout of 500ms and a maximum number of retries, we can ensure that our application remains responsive even in the presence of intermittent errors.
