## Overview of Slopkiller
Slopkiller is an indispensable tool designed to assist in the optimization and improvement of your website's content. This powerful solution is optimized for professionals seeking excellence in their content creation endeavors.

## Technical Features of Slopkiller
- ### Slop Removal
  - **Describe Feature 1**: This feature is instrumental in purging irrelevant or outdated information from your content, ensuring that only relevant, high-quality content remains.
  
- ### Keyword Matching
  - **Describe Feature 2**: Slopkiller also includes a keyword matching tool which helps in identifying and removing any duplicate keywords within the text for improved readability and search engine optimization.

- ### Paragraph Length Adjustment
  - **Describe Feature 3**: This feature is particularly useful when dealing with large masses of content. It automates adjusting paragraph lengths to keep your sentences from getting overly long, enhancing readability and SEO performance.
  
## Usage

#### Step 1: Preparation
1. Import the text file you want to clean into a designated area or use the provided file as an example.
2. Open Slopkiller.
3. Choose `Import Text` to import the content for cleaning.

#### Step 2: Advanced Settings Adjustment
1. Adjust settings by selecting `Options`.
2. Utilize the sliders and checkboxes to fine-tune parameters such as word frequency cutoff (WFC), maximum sentence length, paragraph length adjustment.
3. Set your preferred settings in the advanced options before applying the changes.

#### Step 3: Application of Filters
1. Select your desired filter(s) by clicking on the corresponding buttons or dropdowns.
2. Apply filters to remove irrelevant content, duplicated words, and enhance readability.
