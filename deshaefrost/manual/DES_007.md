# deshaefrost - DES_007

## Overview

`deshaefrost` is a high‑performance personal brand intelligence engine designed specifically for creators and public figures like Deshae Frost. It automates cross‑platform brand consistency auditing, sentiment‑weighted content scheduling, and audience growth forecasting using real‑time social graph analysis.

## Core Features

### 1. Multi‑Platform Brand Consistency Auditor
Scrapes and compares your bio, profile image, pinned content, and hashtag clusters across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and X. It computes a **Brand Cohesion Score (BCS)** on a 0‑100 scale using cosine similarity between embedding vectors of your metadata.  
**Parameters:** `--platforms "ig,tt,yt,x" --bcs-threshold 85 --embed-model "all-MiniLM-L6-v2"`  
**Metric:** Delta detection in under **250 ms** per platform via cached API polling.

### 2. Sentiment‑Weighted Content Scheduler
Analyzes the recent 500 engagements (comments, shares, saves) on your last 30 posts to assign a **Sentiment Volatility Index (SVI)**. The scheduler then auto‑adjusts posting times to avoid negative sentiment peaks, optimizing for a **minimum +12% engagement rate** over a 7‑day rolling window.  
**Parameters:** `--lookback 30 --engagement-weight 0.6 --svi-threshold 0.35`  
**Backend:** PostgreSQL‑backed time‑series storage with millisecond‑precision timestamps for each engagement event.

### 3. Audience Growth Forecaster (AGF)
Uses an LSTM neural network trained on your historical follower acquisition curves and viral event logs. It outputs a **30‑day growth projection** with **±3% RMSE** and identifies the top 3 content themes most likely to trigger a breakout.  
**Parameters:** `--forecast-days 30 --model "lstm_128" --feature-set "follower_rate,share_velocity,hashtag_density"`  
**Output:** JSON with `projected_followers`, `confidence_interval_95`, and `recommended_themes[]`.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Initialize the Brand Audit
Run the following to scan all linked platforms and generate your baseline Brand Cohesion Score:
```bash
deshaefrost audit --platforms "ig,tt,yt,x" --output-format json --save-cache
```
This writes a `brand_cohesion.json` containing per‑platform vectors and a global BCS. The cache reduces subsequent runs to under 1 second.

### Step 2: Schedule with Sentiment Awareness
After the audit, feed the output into the scheduler:
```bash
deshaefrost schedule --input brand_cohesion.json --svi-threshold 0.35 --post-time "2025-06-15T14:00:00Z"
```
The tool will reorder your draft queue (from `drafts/`) based on SVI scores and output a `schedule_plan.csv` with timestamps precise to the millisecond.

### Step 3: Generate a Growth Forecast
To predict your next 30 days and receive content recommendations:
```bash
deshaefrost forecast --forecast-days 30 --feature-set "follower_rate,share_velocity,hashtag_density" --plot
```
The command produces an interactive HTML chart (`forecast_30d.html`) and a `forecast_report.json` with the top 3 theme suggestions (e.g., "behind‑the‑scenes", "collab", "trend‑reaction").

## Best Practices

### Practice 1: Run Weekly Brand Audits with Slack Alerts
Schedule `deshaefrost audit` as a cron job every Monday at 09:00 UTC. Pipe the output to a webhook that posts BCS changes > 5 points to your team’s Slack channel. This catches brand drift before it compounds.

### Practice 2: Combine Scheduler with AGF for Viral Windows
Use the AGF’s `recommended_themes` to pre‑populate your content draft queue, then run the scheduler with `--svi-threshold 0.25` (tighter threshold) during forecasted breakout windows. This ensures you post high‑sentiment content exactly when your audience is most receptive.
