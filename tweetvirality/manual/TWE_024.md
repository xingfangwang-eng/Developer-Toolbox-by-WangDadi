## Overview


### What is tweetvirality?

tweetvirality is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields.

### Core Features

- Tweet Viral Index: A measure of how well tweets are likely to go viral
- Social Media Reach Analysis: Visualizing the reach and engagement potential of individual tweets across different social media platforms
- Viral Engagement Score: Calculating the overall virality (like, share, retweet) of a tweet based on data from Twitter

### How to Use

1. Step 1: Navigate to the "Settings" page under your account dashboard.
2. Step 2: Select the "Viral Index" option within the Viral Metrics section.
3. Step 3: Adjust parameters as necessary to fine-tune your analysis.
