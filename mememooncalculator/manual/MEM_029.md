## Technical Innovation 3: Load Balancing and Circuit Breakers for Scalability

MemoMoonCalculator's architecture incorporates a load balancer to distribute incoming traffic across multiple nodes, ensuring that no single node becomes overwhelmed. This approach enables seamless scalability, allowing the system to handle increasing loads without sacrificing performance.

We also employ circuit breakers to detect and prevent cascading failures in case of node failures or high latency. When a node becomes unresponsive, the circuit breaker trips, redirecting traffic to healthy nodes until the problematic node recovers.

**Code Snippet 3: Load Balancing and Circuit Breaker Implementation**
