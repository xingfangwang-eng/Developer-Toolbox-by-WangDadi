**Core Functionality**

### **Smart Energy Insights**
Our proprietary AI-powered sensors continuously monitor energy consumption patterns in real-time, providing unparalleled visibility into usage trends, anomalies, and potential inefficiencies. With this granular insight, users can identify areas for improvement and optimize their energy use.

Advanced predictive maintenance algorithms detect potential equipment failures before they occur, enabling proactive maintenance and reducing downtime. Our intuitive reporting dashboard provides real-time analytics on energy usage, highlighting opportunities for improvement and progress towards sustainability goals.
