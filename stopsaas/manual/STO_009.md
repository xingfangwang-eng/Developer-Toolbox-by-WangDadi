### What is stopsaas?

### Core Features

- Automates managing and optimizing SaaS subscriptions to minimize costs.
- Provides tools for evaluating and selecting the most effective SaaS tools based on performance metrics.
- Offers robust analytics for monitoring subscription performance, enabling organizations to make informed decisions.

### How to Use

1. Step 1: Connect your SaaS subscriptions to stopsaas
   - Go to "Settings" > "Connect SaaS"
   - Select your preferred SaaS provider and connect it to stopsaas.
2. Step 2: Review and customize subscription settings
   - Go to "Settings" > "Subscription Management"
   - Customize subscription parameters such as pricing, terms, etc.
3. Step 3: Monitor and optimize subscription performance
    - Go to "Performance Metrics" > "Analyze Performance"
    - Analyze key metrics like cost per user, churn rate, customer lifetime value (CLV)
4. Step 4: Evaluate and select the best SaaS tool for your organization
   - Go to "Tool Evaluation" > "Evaluating Tools"
   - Use advanced search filters to find tools that meet your performance criteria.
5. Step 5: Automate subscription management processes
    - Implement rules, alerts or workflows to automate subscription management tasks.
6. Step 6: Monitor and optimize the selected SaaS tool's performance
   - Go to "Tool Management" > "Monitoring"
   - Use tools like Google Analytics to track tool performance.

## Best Practices

- **Monitor Subscription Performance Regularly**: Keep an eye on key metrics such as churn rate, CLV, customer retention.
