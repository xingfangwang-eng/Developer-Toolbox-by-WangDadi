## Overview


### What is manualslib?

manualslib is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields.

### Core Features

- **Search and retrieve product manuals** - A searchable database of industry-specific product user guides.
- **Automated document updates** - Real-time updates for your digital library from the moment you add or update content.
- **Customizable reporting** - Generate reports on how often a particular manual is accessed and recommended by users.

### How to Use

1. Step 1: Open the Manualslib tool in your browser.
2. Step 2: Search for specific product manuals by keyword, manufacturer name, model number or document type.
3. Step 3: Click on the desired document to view the content.

### Best Practices

- **Regularly update your library** - Keep your manual database updated with new documents and regularly review outdated ones to ensure your library remains relevant.
- **Regularly add feedback and comments** - Add notes or provide feedback when you use a product manual to help refine future content for your users.
- **Use the API endpoints for bulk operations** - Utilize APIs to automate tasks such as adding multiple documents, updating document metadata, or searching for specific types of manuals.
