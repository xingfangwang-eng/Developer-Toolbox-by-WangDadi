### What is manualslib?

### Core Features

- **Keyword Indexing**: A feature that helps businesses and organizations manage their product manuals more efficiently.
- **Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Tools**: Automatically generate relevant keywords based on the content of the manual, improving its visibility in search results.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Designed with a user-friendly interface for easy navigation and search functionality.

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Creating a New Manual

1. Go to the manualslib website or use the provided URL (https://manualslib.example.com).
2. Click on the "Create" button.
3. Fill in the necessary information such as the manual title, author, and content description.

#### Step 2: Adding Products to Your Library

1. Once you have created your first manual, click on it to see its details (i.e., the "My Manual" menu).
2. Click on "Add Products".
3. Search for products that are relevant to your manuals by using the "Search" feature.
4. Import them into your library to easily reference and manage all of your product manuals.

#### Step 3: Managing Your Libraries

1. From the "My Library" tab, you can view all of the manual libraries created in the system (i.e., "Manage Libraries").
2. Update or delete manually-generated products.
3. Add new products directly from your library to manage and update all related documents.
