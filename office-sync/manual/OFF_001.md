### How to Use

1. **Create an account**:
   - Go to the `Register` page on our website.
   - Fill out the required fields: username, password, organization name, email address, and a brief description of your office sync needs.

2. **Choose your preferred platform**: Select from desktop or mobile apps that best suit your workflow needs (e.g., macOS, Windows, iOS).
3. **Create shared folders for collaboration and file sharing**: Share documents with specific team members using our shared folder system.
4. **Start syncing**:
   - Sign in to your account on the desktop app or mobile app.
   - Upload files from local storage or directly link them to your account.
   - Sync all available files between devices.

5. **View, edit, and share documents**: Access, edit, and share files with ease across different platforms (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox).

### Best Practices

1. **Regular backup**: Ensure regular backups of important documents on both local storage and cloud services to avoid data loss.
2. **Secure your documents**: Use encryption for sensitive information stored in cloud services.
