** Idempotent Design Patterns**

To ensure the reliability and consistency of our data processing pipeline, MockupNuke incorporates idempotent design patterns throughout its architecture. This allows us to safely retry failed operations without introducing inconsistencies or corrupting data.

In practice, this means that when a task fails, we can simply re-execute it from the last successful state, ensuring that the overall system remains in a consistent and predictable state. By leveraging idempotence, we can design our system to be more fault-tolerant, reducing the need for error-prone manual intervention or costly rollbacks.
