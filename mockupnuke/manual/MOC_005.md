## Expert Advice
### Performance Optimization Recommendation One: Cache-Enabled Rendering
To improve performance and reduce latency, enable cache-enabled rendering in your MockupNuke configuration. This feature stores frequently accessed design elements in memory, reducing the number of requests to our servers and speeding up design rendering.
