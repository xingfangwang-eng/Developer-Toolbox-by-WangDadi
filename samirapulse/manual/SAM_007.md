### What is Samira Pulse?

Samira Pulse, SAM_007's core feature, is a unique platform for tracking heart rate variability (HRV), which is the fluctuation of HR over time. HRV measures your body’s state of alertness and stress, and is closely linked to health outcomes. By monitoring HRV, you can assess your overall well-being and tailor your wellness plan accordingly.
