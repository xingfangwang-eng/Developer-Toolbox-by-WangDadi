### Best Practices

#### Practice 1: Setting SMART Goals
- **Specific**: Set clear, measurable objectives for improvement.
- **Measurable**: Use metrics to track progress towards these goals.
- **Achievable**: Ensure that the goals are realistic and attainable within a set timeframe.
- **Relevant**: Align your goals with your overall health and wellness plan.
- **Time-bound**: Establish deadlines or milestones for tracking progress.
