### What is NoiseKiller?

NoiseKiller is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields. It was created to improve the efficiency of your work by reducing noise and clutter in your Slack chats. With its advanced algorithms, it can detect unnecessary information in chat conversations and filter out irrelevant details.

### Core Features

- ### Filtered Chat Conversations: 

  * Detects and removes redundant or irrelevant information from messages.
  * Filters out noise related to personal communications or unrelated discussion topics.
  * Enhances the quality of your Slack chats by improving their clarity and focus on relevant content.

- ### AI Context Window Optimizer:

  * Automates the creation of context windows based on user-defined parameters, making it easy to track ongoing conversations across different channels.
  * Enables users to save time in managing large datasets by optimizing the context window size for efficiency.
  * Supports multi-channel data analysis and provides insights into communication patterns.

- ### LLM Prompt Compression:

  * Compacts long or complex prompts using AI techniques to reduce their length.
  * Assists users in presenting their ideas concisely, enhancing productivity and streamlining the writing process.
  * Utilizes advanced algorithms to optimize content and simplify complex instructions for better understanding.

### How to Use

1. **Install NoiseKiller:**

   - Navigate to the `NoiseKiller` folder located within your Slack workspace.
   - Right-click in your chat history or anywhere on the screen, select "Open with", then "NoiseKiller".

2. **Customize Your Settings:** 

   - Access the settings menu from the top right corner of your Slack interface.

3. **Filter Chat Conversations:**

   - Choose the chat conversation you want to filter in the left sidebar.
   - Click on the "Filter" button or use keyboard shortcut (Ctrl + F).

4. **Track Context Windows:** 

   - To create a context window, click "Context Window" from the left sidebar. You can choose between different types of context windows (e.g., historical conversations, ongoing discussions).
   - Adjust parameters such as time range and conversation channel.

5. **Optimize LLM Prompts:**

   - Use the "LLM Prompt Optimization" feature for compacting long or complex prompts.
   - This feature compresses text using AI algorithms to create short, concise messages suitable for sharing with colleagues.
