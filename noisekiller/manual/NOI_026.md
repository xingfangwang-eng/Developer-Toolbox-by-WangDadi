### What is NoiseKiller?

NoiseKiller is a professional tool designed to optimize your Slack chats and improve communication efficiency. It utilizes advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques to enhance the quality of your Slack conversations.

### Core Features

- **Slack Chat Cleaner**: Removes distracting noise, such as irrelevant messages or notifications, from your chat history.
- **AI Context Window Optimizer**: Utilizes AI to suggest the most relevant information in response to user queries, enhancing communication accuracy and efficiency.
- **LLM Prompt Compression**: Reduces the size of your LLM prompts by optimizing responses for context, ensuring faster turnaround times.

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Install and Import NoiseKiller
1. Go to the official website [NoiseKiller](https://noisekiller.example.com).
2. Sign up or sign in using your preferred credentials.
3. Once logged in, select "Install" under "Features."
4. After installation, navigate to the "Settings" tab and connect your Slack workspace.

#### Step 2: Configure NoiseKiller
1. Go to "Settings > Chat Optimization."
2. Enable "NoiseKiller" for all channels you want optimized.
3. Customize the settings based on your preferences for noise reduction and context optimization.

#### Step 3: Trigger NoiseKiller Automations
1. Navigate to the "Settings > Automation" in the main menu.
2. Enable the automated trigger within a specific channel or workspace.
3. Configure the timings and frequency of triggering your automation to maintain optimal chat conditions.

### Best Practices

- **Practice 1**: Regularly use NoiseKiller by setting reminders for its usage during critical Slack meetings.
- **Practice 2**: Keep the settings of noise reduction and context optimization flexible, adjusting them according to the specific needs of each Slack channel or workspace.
