# noisekiller - NOI_004

## Overview

NoiseKiller is a professional tool designed to optimize your Slack chat experience. It helps you to filter out spam and irrelevant messages, providing you with a cleaner and more focused environment for important conversations.

### Core Features

1. **Chat Cleaner**: Filter out irrelevant messages from your Slack chat history.
2. **AI Context Window Optimizer**: Improve the AI context window of your chosen LLMs by optimizing their prompts.
3. **LLM Prompt Compression**: Reduce the size of your model’s prompt, ensuring better completion rates and reducing cognitive load.

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Install NoiseKiller
1. Go to the [official documentation](https://noisekiller.example.com/docs) for detailed installation instructions.
2. Follow the installation guide provided there.

#### Step 2: Optimize Chat History
1. Access your Slack workspace by logging in and navigating to your channels.
2. Use the `NoiseKiller` command within a channel or select the `NoiseKiller` option from the top menu.
3. The tool will display your chat history with relevant messages highlighted for you.

#### Step 3: Improve AI Context Window
1. Navigate to the settings in your Slack workspace and locate the LLMs section.
2. Choose an AI model that suits your needs.
3. Adjust the prompts within your chosen models' context windows to improve their performance.

### Best Practices

1. **Regular Maintenance**: Keep NoiseKiller updated by visiting the official documentation for the latest updates.
2. **Personalized Settings**: Optimize your chat history and AI context window settings based on personal preferences.
