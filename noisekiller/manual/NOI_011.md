### What is NoiseKiller?

NoiseKiller is an innovative AI-powered solution that not only helps you filter out unnecessary information but also optimizes your Slack chat experience, ensuring that key messages stand out among the noise. By utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms, NoiseKiller offers a unique blend of features to help users streamline their workflows and enhance productivity.

### Core Features

- **Chat Filter**: Our powerful AI filters are capable of identifying the most relevant content in your Slack channel, helping you focus on what's important.
- **AI Context Window Optimizer**: With NoiseKiller, you can adjust the context window for optimal performance. This tool optimizes your context window based on usage patterns and feedback from users, ensuring that conversations remain focused and relevant.
- **Prompt Compression**: Enhance your LLM responses with NoiseKiller's compression feature, optimizing them for speed and efficiency.

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Install and Activate
1. Open your preferred Slack chat channel.
2. Navigate to the "Settings" tab within the chat window.
3. Select "Tools & Features" from the menu options.
4. Within the Tools & Features section, look for the NoiseKiller icon. Click on it to activate the tool.

#### Step 2: Enable AI Context Window
1. Locate the AI context window settings in the top left corner of your chat channel.
2. Select "Settings."
3. Scroll through the options and adjust the context window size, color scheme, or even customize the font for a more personalized experience.
4. Once satisfied with the settings, click on "Apply."

#### Step 3: Start Filtering and Optimizing Your Conversations
1. As you engage in a conversation within the channel, look for relevant messages that can be filtered out by NoiseKiller's filters.
2. By adjusting the context window size, color scheme, or even font style settings as needed, optimize your Slack chat experience.
