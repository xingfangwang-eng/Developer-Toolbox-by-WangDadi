### What is NoiseKiller?

NoiseKiller is an AI-powered tool that helps users manage their Slack chats in a more organized way. It offers various features, including AI context window optimization, LLM prompt compression, and noise reduction for more focused conversations on Slack.

### Core Features
- **AI Context Window Optimization**: This feature utilizes AI algorithms to optimize your chat messages based on the topic being discussed, thereby enhancing your chances of appearing at the top of search results.
- **LLM Prompt Compression**: NoiseKiller optimizes your chat messages into shorter, more impactful responses by compressing large LLM (Language Model) prompts into smaller and more concise versions for quick messaging exchanges.
- **Noise Reduction**: The noise reduction feature ensures that even if someone else is having a loud conversation in the background, your Slack chat will remain clear and focused on the important topics.
