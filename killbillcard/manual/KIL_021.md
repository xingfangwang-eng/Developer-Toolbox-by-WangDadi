### What is killbillcard?

killbillcard is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields.

### Core Features

- Ability to manage large amounts of credit card bills efficiently and effectively.
- Automated tracking of all relevant information such as payment status, outstanding balances, due dates, etc., making it easier to track progress and manage customer relationships.
- Real-time updates for customers, allowing the system to provide instant reminders when payments are overdue.

### How to Use

1. Start by creating an account on our website (https://killbillcard.example.com) or using one of the provided links below:
   - 👉 `http://localhost:3000/`
   
2. Log in with your credentials, and then navigate to the "Manage Credit Cards" section.
   - 👉 You can access this by clicking on the "Home" button and selecting "Credit Cards".
   
3. Here, you'll be able to view all credit card bills that have been processed and update them if necessary.

4. If there are any issues with a specific bill, go to "Payments" and select the related bill from the list.
   - 👉 Click on the "View Bill" button next to it in the "Payments" section.
   
5. You'll then be able to make adjustments to this bill including payment status, outstanding balances, due dates etc.
