### What is killbillcard?

killbillcard is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields.

### Core Features

- Analyze the payment history of customers and detect any suspicious transactions.
- Compare the credit card bills of different merchants to identify fraudulent activities.
- Track the payment status of each bill separately for better customer service experience.
- Automate billing processes, reducing manual errors and improving efficiency.

### How to Use

1. Step 1: Click on "Start Analysis" button in the top left corner.
2. Step 2: Enter your card number and merchant name.
3. Step 3: Review the results and make any necessary changes to the bill processing process.

### Best Practices

- Practice 1: Regularly update your credit card details for better security.
- Practice 2: Use killbillcard to review all your credit card bills at once, saving time and reducing errors.
