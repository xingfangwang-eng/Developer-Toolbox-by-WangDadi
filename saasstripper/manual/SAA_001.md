# saasstripper - SAA_001

## Overview

Introducing our new SaaS cost reduction and management tool, designed specifically for organizations looking to optimize their subscription costs. By streamlining the process of managing SaaS subscriptions, saasstripper helps businesses reduce overall SaaS spend and improve financial performance.

## Core Features

- **Subscription Management**: Simplifies the process of adding, updating, and removing subscriptions from your ecosystem.
- **Cost Analysis**: Analyzes subscription spend to identify areas for cost reduction.
- **Efficient Billing**: Automates billing processes with flexible payment options, including automatic invoicing and auto-renewal based on usage.

## How to Use

1. **Start by Creating or Adding a Subscription**:
   - On the Dashboard, click "Create Subscriptions" to start adding new SaaS subscriptions.
   - Select the relevant application for your business needs from the available list of applications.
   - Enter details such as subscription name, billing information (including price and currency), and any custom fields that apply.

2. **Review and Update Subscription Details**:
   - Once a subscription is created, you can edit or update its details at any time by navigating to "Manage Subscriptions."

3. **Remove a Subscription**:
   - You can remove a SaaS subscription from your ecosystem by going to the "Manage Subscriptions" section of the dashboard.
   - Select the subscription and click on the "Delete Subscription" option.

## Best Practices

- **Regular Review**: Ensure you keep track of all subscriptions regularly, as this is key to effective cost reduction and monitoring.

- **Update Pricing Model**: If prices change or there are new pricing models available, promptly adjust your subscription management processes.
