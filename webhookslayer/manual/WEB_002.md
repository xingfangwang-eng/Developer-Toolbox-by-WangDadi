### What is webhookslayer?

webhookslayer is an AI-driven tool designed to simulate the behavior of real-world conditions in order to test the reliability of your webhooks. The platform simulates a wide range of network environments and uses advanced techniques, including exponential backoff testing and Stripe Webhook Retry Logic, to ensure that your webhooks are resilient under various load scenarios.

### Core Features

- **Exponential Backoff Testing**: webhookslayer provides an exhaustive exploration of potential failure points in your system. It simulates a series of failures by repeatedly retrying the connection until it is established or fails at a certain threshold.
- **Stripe Webhook Retry Logic**: The platform includes a module that automatically retries failed webhooks based on Stripe's rules for handling webhook failures, which can vary depending on the specific account settings and the type of error encountered.
