### What is webhookslayer?

webhookslayer is a professional tool designed to enhance the reliability of webhooks, making it easier for developers to handle complex events. It implements strategies such as Exponential Backoff Tester and Stripe Webhook Retry Logic, ensuring that your applications can recover quickly from disruptions.

### Core Features

- **Webhook Reliability Simulator**: This feature simulates a high frequency of webhook requests, allowing you to test the resilience of your webhook endpoint in real-world scenarios. It evaluates the impact of various conditions on webhooks' performance.
- **Exponential Backoff Tester**: Utilizing an exponential backoff strategy, this feature calculates and provides a recommended backoff period for sending future webhook requests to recover from intermittent failures more effectively.
- **Stripe Webhook Retry Logic**: Implementing Stripe's retry logic, webhookslayer ensures that any attempts to trigger a webhook through the Stripe API will be retried in case of failure until successful. This approach helps maintain an active connection with Stripe without requiring a full payment for every transaction.
