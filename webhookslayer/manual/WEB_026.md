### Detailed Content

#### Feature 1: Exponential Backoff Tester
**Feature Overview:** 
The **Exponential Backoff Tester** module within webhookslayer helps simulate backoff behavior during retries. This feature is crucial when testing the reliability of Webhooks, ensuring that the system adjusts its response rate based on the number of failed attempts.

#### Feature 2: Stripe Webhook Retry Logic
**Feature Overview:** 
The **Stripe Webhook Retry Logic** module in webhookslayer simulates the retry policy adopted by Stripe for their Webhooks. This feature is particularly useful for businesses that rely heavily on Stripe's payment gateway and need to ensure that all requests are handled efficiently and consistently.

#### Feature 3: Webhook Reliability Simulator
**Feature Overview:** 
The **Webhook Reliability Simulator** module in webhookslayer acts as a testbed, simulating the behavior of various webhooks during their lifecycle. This feature allows developers to validate the expected outcomes of different scenarios, helping identify potential issues and optimizing Webhook handling strategies.
