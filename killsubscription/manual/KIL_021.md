### What is killsubscription?

killsubscription is an innovative product that provides users with flexible and efficient tools for managing their subscriptions. ### Core Features

- **Plan Management**: Manage multiple subscription plans including pricing, discounts, and usage limits.
- **Cost Control**: Track the cost per user or per plan, identify areas for savings, and optimize spending on subscriptions.
- **Usage Tracking**: Analyze usage patterns to predict future costs and make informed decisions about service renewals.
