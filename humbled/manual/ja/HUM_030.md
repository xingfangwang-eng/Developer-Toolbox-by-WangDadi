**humbled**
===============

### AI-driven Adaptive Learning Platform

humbled is an AI-driven adaptive learning platform designed to help individuals and organizations quickly master new technologies and skills. The platform uses machine learning algorithms and artificial intelligence technology to analyze user behavior and knowledge requirements, providing personalized learning recommendations and resources.

### Core Functions
-------------------

#### **AI-driven Learning Path Generation**

humbled AI utilizes natural language processing and image recognition technologies to analyze users' learning needs and interests, generating personalized learning paths. The platform also correlates learning paths with relevant resources, video tutorials, and exam questions, providing a comprehensive learning experience.

#### **Smart Assessment**

humbled AI uses machine learning algorithms to assess user assignments, automatically marking correct answers and providing feedback information. The platform can adjust learning paths and difficulty levels based on users' learning effects and progress, helping them better master new technologies and skills.

#### **Community Sharing**

The humbled platform encourages user interaction and sharing. Users can publish their learning achievements, experiences, and ideas on the platform and engage in discussions with other users. This fosters a deeper understanding of new technologies and skills and enables users to benefit from others' experiences.
