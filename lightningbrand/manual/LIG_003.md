### What is lightningbrand?

lightningbrand is a professional tool that helps solve problems in related fields.

### Core Features

- **Keyword Extraction**: Automatically extracts important keywords from text.
- **Content Curation**: Creates a curated list of content to improve SEO and reach a broader audience.
- **Content Ranking**: Calculates the importance of each piece of content based on its relevance.

### How to Use

1. Step 1: Keyword Extraction
   - Open the lightningbrand tool.
   - Paste your text into the text box.
   - Click "Extract Keywords."

2. Step 2: Content Curation
   - Choose a category for your content.
   - Enter the URL of the website you want to curate.
   - Click "Curate" to get a curated list.

3. Step 3: Content Ranking
   - Use the ranking algorithm to determine the importance of each piece of content.
   - View the results in the rankings page.

### Best Practices

- **Regular Updates**: Ensure your keyword extraction and curation features are up-to-date with the latest industry trends and keywords.
- **Customizable Settings**: Allow users to customize their settings, such as the number of keywords to extract or the frequency of content updates.
