- **Audio Compression**: Enables efficient compression of audio files while maintaining clear sound quality.
- **Audio Optimization**: Enhances audio data efficiency through various optimization techniques.

### How to Use

1. **Step 1**: **Connect to your Audio Files**
   - Ensure you have a file or directory of audio files that need editing and compression. These can be WAV, MP3, or other formats supported by `slimsnd`.
2. **Step 2**: **Select Edit Mode**
   - Choose the appropriate mode based on your requirements—trimming, normalizing, reverberation, etc.

   - Customize settings according to editing preferences. For example, adjust volume, trim duration, or apply effects like reverb.

   - Select the compression method (LAME, ffmpeg-based), choose an output format, and configure quality adjustments for best results.

   - Once satisfied with your edits and compression, save your changes.
