## Overview

pingthemio is a powerful network monitoring tool that helps in troubleshooting and resolving issues related to internet connectivity.

### What does it do?

Our Network Ping Tool (Pingthemio) efficiently checks for any latency problems between your devices. It quickly determines the status of your network, ensuring smooth communication with other systems.

### Core Features

- **Internet Connection Test**: Instantly checks if your network is up and running.
- **Server Monitoring**: Monitors your servers' performance, providing real-time insights into their health and connectivity status.
- **Packet Loss Detection**: Identifies any issues related to packet loss, helping in improving overall internet speed.

### How to use

1. **Step 1: Ping Your Network**
   - Open pingthemio and select the network to test.
   - Enter a single number, such as `80`, to send ICMP requests to check server connectivity.

2. **Step 2: View Results**
   - Once sent, click on the "Results" tab to view detailed information about the connection status.
   - Check any error codes or messages displayed for further troubleshooting.

3. **Step 3: Utilize Reports**
   - If the ping fails, review your reports for any potential reasons or solutions that may have been missed in other steps.
