## Overview
In today's digital age, there is an increasing demand for high-quality content on various platforms. This project aims to help notetakers and content creators optimize their content for search engines (SEO) by automatically cleaning up their documents before publishing them on Notion or any other document management platform.

## Detailed Content

### What Is Notiontablecleaner?
Notiontablecleaner is a powerful software tool designed specifically for those who need to improve the SEO of their documents. It automates various processes such as keyword optimization, content cleanup, and more to help notetakers and content creators create better content that ranks higher in search engines.

### Core Features
- **Keyword Optimization**: Notiontablecleaner helps ensure your document's title, headings, and other keywords are correctly formatted for SEO.
- **Content Cleanup**: Notiontablecleaner automatically removes irrelevant data, adds relevant metadata (e.g., dates), and updates file names to improve SEO-friendly filenames.

### How to Use
1. **Start by Creating a Document**:
   - Open your Notion document editor and create a new section or page.
2. **Enable Notiontablecleaner from the Tools menu**:
   - Click on "Tools" in the top navigation menu and select "NotionTableCleaner."
3. **Select Your Document**: Once enabled, you will see the NotionTableCleaner icon with an option to "Review and Clean Up."

4. **Start the Cleaning Process**: Click on the "Clean Up" button to initiate the cleaning process.
5. **Customize Settings**:
   - Adjust settings for keyword optimization or content cleanup as desired.
6. **Save Changes and Publish Your Document**:
   - Once satisfied with the cleaned-up document, click on "Publish."

### Best Practices
- **Regular Updates**: Ensure Notiontablecleaner is updated regularly to take advantage of new SEO features.
- **Monitor and Analyze Results**: Regularly review your documents' performance in search engine results using tools like Google Search Console.
