### **Distributed Lock Support**

Our tool integrates distributed lock support, ensuring that multiple threads or processes access shared data in a synchronized manner. This technology prevents data contention, locking, and deadlocks, thereby enhancing system reliability and performance.

When using our distributed lock mechanism, we generate a unique lock identifier, encrypt it, and perform a hash operation to ensure its security and uniqueness. Additionally, we provide an intelligent lock management system that dynamically adjusts lock timeouts and priorities based on actual conditions.
