## How to Use

### Step 1: Configure the Festival Radar and Set Viral Thresholds
- Navigate to the **Radar** tab and connect your social accounts (TikTok, Instagram, X, Reddit).
- Define your target keywords (e.g., "Coachella 2025," "Sahara Tent," "Beyoncé surprise").
- Set a **Viral Threshold** — a minimum engagement velocity (e.g., 500 likes/min) that triggers an alert.
- Enable **Auto-Capture**: coachellaviral will automatically pull the top 3 trending clips from the festival feed every 10 minutes.

### Step 2: Run the AI Remixer on Captured Content
- From the **Radar Feed**, select a clip or upload your own raw footage (MP4, MOV, or direct URL).
- In the **Remixer** panel, choose output platforms (e.g., TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts).
- Toggle **Style Transfer**: select from presets like "Festival Vibe," "Cinematic Slow-Mo," or "Meme Format."
- Hit **Generate**. The system returns 12+ variants within 90 seconds. Preview each variant in the built-in player.

### Step 3: Deploy with Smart Syndication and Monitor Performance
- Review the generated variants in the **Queue** tab. coachellaviral auto-selects the best-performing version based on predicted virality score.
- Click **Schedule** to set posting times (e.g., "Post at 10 AM PST, 2 PM PST, and 8 PM PST").
- Enable **Auto-Promote**: if a variant exceeds your viral threshold (e.g., 10K views in 1 hour), coachellaviral will automatically boost it with a paid ad budget from your connected ad accounts.
- Monitor the **Dashboard** for real-time metrics: views, shares, sentiment, and estimated earned media value (EMV).
