## Detailed Content

### What is coachellaviral?

coachellaviral is a professional, data-intensive platform that solves the fragmentation problem in festival viral marketing. It automates the discovery, creation, and distribution of high-engagement content across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts, specifically targeting Coachella's unique audience demographics. Instead of manual guesswork, it uses NLP-driven sentiment analysis to predict virality before posts go live.

### Core Features

1. **Predictive Virality Engine (PVE)** : An AI model that analyzes historical Coachella content patterns (hashtag velocity, audio spikes, influencer cross-references) to score your draft content's viral potential in real-time. It outputs a "V-Score" between 0-100 with actionable optimization tips.

2. **Cross-Platform Content Matrix** : A unified dashboard that lets you schedule, remix, and repurpose a single piece of content (e.g., a 15-second TikTok clip) into platform-optimized formats for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and Pinterest Pins—all with auto-generated captions and SEO metadata.

3. **Live Festival Heatmap** : A geo-temporal visualization layer that overlays real-time social media activity on the Coachella grounds. It highlights which stages, art installations, or surprise guests are trending right now, enabling you to pivot your content strategy mid-festival for maximum engagement.

### How to Use

1. **Initialize a Campaign with Trend Analysis** : Navigate to the "Campaigns" tab and click "New Campaign." Select "Coachella 2024" as the festival template. The system will automatically pull the top 50 trending hashtags and audio tracks from TikTok and Instagram. Use the "Trend Score" filter to isolate content with >80% growth rate in the last 24 hours. Set your target audience by age, location, and device type.

2. **Generate and Optimize Content Assets** : Upload your raw footage (e.g., a 4K clip of a live set). The PVE will analyze it and suggest a "Viral Hook" (e.g., "Start with the crowd reaction at 0:03"). Use the "Auto-Caption" tool to generate platform-specific captions with emoji and hashtag density optimized for Instagram's algorithm. Then, click "Remix" to create a 9:16 vertical version for TikTok and a 16:9 horizontal version for YouTube Shorts.

3. **Schedule and Monitor with Real-Time Alerts** : Set your posting schedule using the "Golden Hour" feature, which analyzes your audience's timezone data (e.g., 10 PM PST for West Coast Gen Z). Enable "Viral Alert" to get a push notification when your content's engagement rate spikes above 15% in the first hour. Use the "Heatmap" to see if your post is being shared from a specific area (e.g., the Sahara Tent) and adjust your on-the-ground strategy accordingly.

### Best Practices

- **Leverage the "Pre-Festival Buzz" Window** : Start your campaign 14 days before Coachella. Use the PVE to identify under-served keywords (e.g., "Coachella outfit hacks 2024" or "RV camping tips"). Schedule teaser content with countdown stickers. This builds algorithmic authority before the main event, ensuring your posts rank higher when search volume explodes.

- **Run A/B Tests on Audio Selection** : Don't rely on trending songs alone. Use the "Audio Explorer" to test two versions of the same video—one with a viral track and one with a niche, festival-specific remix. Monitor the "Share Rate" metric. Often, niche audio outperforms because it reduces competition in the recommendation algorithm. Archive losing variants and double down on winners.
