## Core Features

### 1. Festival Trend Pulse Engine
Real-time ingestion of Coachella-specific hashtags, audio snippets, and visual motifs across 10+ platforms. The engine identifies emerging viral patterns (e.g., "desert fit check" or "stage rush edits") within 90 seconds of first detection, then auto-generates content briefs with timing windows and competitive gap analysis.

### 2. Multi-Platform Content Syndicator
One-click deployment of festival clips with platform-native formatting (9:16, 1:1, 16:9) and metadata injection. Automatically rewrites captions using festival-specific slang and emoji patterns, adjusts hashtag density per platform algorithm, and schedules posts during peak engagement windows derived from historical Coachella data.

### 3. Viral Score Predictor (VSP)
Proprietary ML model trained on 200,000+ music festival clips. Input a raw video or text concept, and VSP outputs a 0–100 virality probability score, with granular breakdowns by platform, time-of-day, and audience segment. Includes actionable optimization recommendations (e.g., "Add 3-second hook at 0:00–0:03 to boost retention by 34%").

## How to Use

### Step 1: Inject Festival Context into the Engine
Navigate to the **Trend Pulse** dashboard and select "Coachella 2024" from the event dropdown. Paste your raw content URL or upload a video file. The engine will auto-extract metadata (duration, resolution, audio fingerprint) and cross-reference it against active viral patterns. You'll see a live heatmap showing which motifs (e.g., "sundown crowd shots" or "artist meetup moments") are currently under-served.

### Step 2: Optimize and Syndicate via the Content Canvas
Click "Open in Canvas" to access the multi-track editor. Here, you can trim clips to platform-specific max lengths (TikTok: 60s, Reels: 90s, Shorts: 60s). Use the **Viral Score Predictor** button on any segment to get real-time optimization feedback. For example, if VSP flags a "low retention zone" at 0:12–0:15, the system will suggest inserting a fast-cut or text overlay. Once satisfied, hit "Syndicate" to auto-deploy to connected accounts.

### Step 3: Monitor and Iterate with Performance Analytics
After syndication, the **Viral Pulse Dashboard** tracks each clip’s performance across platforms. Metrics include: velocity (likes per hour), share-to-view ratio, and comment sentiment. Use the "Competitive Benchmark" tab to compare your clip’s trajectory against top-performing Coachella content from the same hour. If a clip underperforms, the engine auto-generates a "re-edit plan" with specific changes (e.g., "Shorten intro by 2 seconds, add trending audio overlay").
