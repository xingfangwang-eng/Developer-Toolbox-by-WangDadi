## Core Features

### 1. **Real-Time Sentiment-Driven Content Seeding**
coachellaviral scrapes X (Twitter), TikTok, and Reddit for emerging Coachella-related sentiment shifts, then auto-generates and schedules micro-content (memes, short clips, polls) to ride the wave before it peaks. No manual monitoring, no latency, just algorithmic timing.

### 2. **Cross-Platform Viral Loop Detector**
Our engine identifies viral loops—moments where a single piece of content is being reshared across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube simultaneously. It then injects your branded assets (hashtags, overlays, CTAs) into the loop, converting organic virality into owned traffic. Think of it as a parasitic growth layer for festival content.

### 3. **Festival-Specific SEO Schema Injection**
We auto-generate structured data (JSON-LD) optimized for Coachella-related queries—lineup announcements, set times, after-parties, and travel hacks. This ensures your content ranks for zero-click searches, featured snippets, and Google Discover slots during peak festival season.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Configure Viral Trigger Channels
Navigate to the **Dashboard > Trigger Sources** section. Add your target platforms (e.g., TikTok, Reddit r/Coachella, X lists). Set a sentiment threshold (e.g., “positive sentiment > 70% within 10 minutes of a post”). coachellaviral will then monitor these channels in real-time, flagging any content that crosses the threshold.

### Step 2: Deploy a Viral Loop Injection
In the **Campaigns** tab, select “Viral Loop Injection.” Define your brand asset (e.g., a 15-second video overlay with your URL). Set the injection frequency (e.g., “inject into the top 5% of reshared content every 2 hours”). The engine will automatically detect loops and insert your asset without disrupting the original content’s organic flow.

### Step 3: Optimize SEO Schema for Festival Queries
Go to **SEO Tools > Schema Builder**. Select “Festival Event” as the schema type. Input your target keywords (e.g., “Coachella 2025 lineup,” “Coachella camping hacks”). The tool will generate a JSON-LD snippet. Paste this into your site’s `<head>` tag. coachellaviral will auto-update the schema as new festival data (e.g., lineup changes) is detected.

## Best Practices

### 1. **Target Micro-Moments, Not Macro Trends**
Don’t wait for the main festival weekend. Use coachellaviral’s sentiment detector to target pre-festival anxiety (e.g., “what to pack,” “how to survive the heat”) and post-festival nostalgia (e.g., “best moments,” “after-party locations”). These micro-moments have higher conversion rates because they’re emotionally charged.

### 2. **Cross-Pollinate Viral Loops with UGC**
When the engine detects a viral loop, don’t just inject your brand—inject a call-to-action for user-generated content (e.g., “Tag us in your Coachella fit for a chance to be featured”). This turns a one-time injection into a self-sustaining loop, as users create new content that feeds back into the engine.
