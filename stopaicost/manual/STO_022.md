### Core Features

- **AI Cost Control** 
  - Automates the management of costs associated with AI investments.
  - Streamlines budgeting processes to ensure accurate forecasting of future expenses.
  - Provides real-time visibility into all aspects of AI-related expenditures, helping businesses stay on top of their financial health. 

- **AI Resource Optimization**  
  - Analyzes resource utilization within and across the entire organization.
  - Identifies opportunities for efficient use of hardware, software, and personnel resources dedicated to AI operations.
  - Enhances ROI by identifying areas where investments in AI can be made most effectively.

- **AI Cost Analysis** 
  - Offers a comprehensive analysis of all AI-related costs, including operational expenses, maintenance costs, and potential costs related to data processing.
  - Provides detailed insights into how each cost is contributing to the overall financial health of the business.
