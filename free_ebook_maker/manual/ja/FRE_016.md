**free_ebook_maker**

free_ebook_maker is cutting-edge technology software that automates the creation of books, bringing significant benefits to publishers and artists.


**核心機能** (Core Function)

### 高度なAI駆動自動生成 (Advanced AI-driven Automated Generation)

Our system is equipped with advanced AI technology, which enables automatic recognition of book content, optimal arrangement of articles, and image placement. This allows publishers to create books at an efficiency rate 50 times higher than traditional manual methods.

### 環境汚染低減型PDF生成 (Environmentally Friendly PDF Generation)

free_ebook_maker can automatically generate environmentally friendly PDFs, eliminating the need for book duplication and reducing environmental burden.

### AI駆動画像自動配置 (AI-driven Image Auto-Placement)

Our system uses AI to optimize image placement in books, allowing publishers to create books at a rate 5 times higher than traditional manual methods.


**快速開始** (Quick Start)

### 步骤一：インストール設定 (Step 1: Installation Settings)

1. Install free_ebook_maker on your PC.
2. Generate and customize settings files according to the publisher's requirements.

### 步骤二：初期化 (Step 2: Initialization)

1. Launch free_ebook_maker and load book content.
2. Enable AI-driven automated generation features.

### 步骤三：実行検証 (Step 3: Execution Verification)

1. Generate a PDF automatically.
2. Adjust the generated PDF to make it suitable for actual publication.
