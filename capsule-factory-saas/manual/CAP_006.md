# capsule-factory-saas - CAP_006

## Overview

Capsule Factory SaaS is a production-grade, multi-tenant platform engineered to streamline capsule manufacturing workflows, from raw material tracking to batch release. Built for pharma and nutraceutical factories, it eliminates manual data silos and ensures GMP compliance out of the box.

## Core Features

### 1. Real-Time Batch Genealogy with Immutable Audit Logs
Every capsule batch is traced from raw material lot to finished good. Our blockchain-anchored audit trail captures every temperature excursion, weight deviation, and operator action. No more spreadsheets—just a single, tamper-proof source of truth for FDA and EU GMP audits.

### 2. AI-Driven Capsule Weight & Fill Optimization
Leverage on-device ML models that analyze fill weight variance across 10,000+ capsules per minute. The system auto-adjusts dosing parameters in real time, reducing overfill waste by up to 18% and ensuring USP <698> compliance without manual intervention.

### 3. Multi-Tenant Production Scheduler with Dependency Graph
Unlike generic ERP schedulers, this engine models capsule-specific constraints: gelatin curing time, humidity windows, and encapsulation machine availability. It generates a dependency-aware production plan that minimizes changeover downtime and maximizes OEE.

## How to Use

### 1. Configure Your Capsule Recipe & Material Bill
Navigate to the **Recipe Manager** and define your capsule formulation: shell composition (gelatin, HPMC, pullulan), fill material, and target weight. Use the **Material Lot Scanner** to link supplier certificates of analysis (CoA) to each batch. The system will automatically flag any lot that falls outside your spec limits.

### 2. Initiate a Production Run with Real-Time Monitoring
From the **Production Dashboard**, select your recipe and enter desired batch size. The scheduler will lock the required encapsulation machine and assign a unique batch ID. During production, the **Weight Variance Dashboard** updates every 100ms—hover on any outlier to see the exact timestamp and operator ID. If variance exceeds your threshold, the system triggers an audible alert and pauses the line.

### 3. Run the Post-Batch Compliance Report
Once the batch is complete, click **Generate e-Batch Record**. This produces a PDF containing: raw material lot numbers, in-process weight logs, temperature/humidity curves, and final release test results. The report is automatically signed with a digital timestamp and archived in your **Audit Vault** for 10 years.

## Best Practices

### 1. Pre-Validate Your Recipe Against Regulatory Rules
Before scaling to production, use the built-in **Compliance Checker** to verify your recipe against CFR 21 Part 211 (US) or EU GMP Annex 1. This catches common issues like incompatible shell polymers or missing dissolution tests. We’ve seen factories reduce validation cycles by 40% just by running this check.

### 2. Set Up Automated Alerts for Environmental Drift
Capsule shell integrity is highly sensitive to humidity. In your **Environment Settings**, define a threshold (e.g., 45%–55% RH). Enable the **Slack/Teams webhook** so that when the drying room drifts outside this range, your production lead gets a real-time alert—not a post-mortem email. This alone can prevent batch rejection due to shell brittleness.
